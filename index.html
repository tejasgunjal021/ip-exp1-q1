Amazon

Amazon is a multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. Founded by Jeff Bezos on July 5, 1994, Amazon is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale. It is the largest Internet company by revenue in the world and one of the world's most valuable companies. Amazon has a diverse range of products and services, including Amazon Prime, which offers exclusive access to movies, TV shows, and more. Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a leader in cloud computing solutions, and Amazon Alexa provides voice-activated assistance. Additionally, Amazon Fresh offers grocery delivery, and Amazon Music allows users to stream millions of songs. The company's commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has made it a household name worldwide.
